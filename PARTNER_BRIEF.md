# DemandOrchestrator — Partner Brief (Living Draft)

## Positioning
DemandOrchestrator is an enterprise-ready social operations platform designed for teams that need both **speed** and **governance**.

## The Problem We Solve
Most social tools optimize for posting volume, not operational control. Companies get:
- fragmented workflows
- inconsistent approvals
- unclear publishing authority
- weak auditability

## What Makes DemandOrchestrator Different
- Fast idea-to-publish workflow
- Corporate account model with premium governance
- Explicit publish authorization by platform/account
- Role-based control with approval flows
- Security-first architecture (token auth, hardened sessions, fail-closed checks)

## Product Structure
### Personal Plan
Built for solo operators/creators:
- lower cost
- streamlined publishing flow
- minimal governance overhead

### Corporate Plan (Premium)
Built for teams and organizations:
- org-level access model
- role and permission controls
- domain-aware access policy
- approval + audit controls
- enterprise-style governance features

## Why This Matters to Partners
DemandOrchestrator is not just a scheduler. It is the control layer for social publishing in environments where brand/compliance risk matters.

## Current Build Status
- Token-based auth and session lifecycle implemented
- Corporate vs personal account split implemented
- Entitlements framework implemented and actively enforced
- Security hardening baseline in place
- Continuous blueprint documentation process in place

## Pilot Offer
We recommend a 2–4 week guided pilot:
1. connect priority channels
2. configure account-type and governance policies
3. measure time-to-publish and error reduction
4. validate fit for scale rollout

## Partnership Use Cases
- Strategic distribution partner
- White-label or co-branded deployment
- Enterprise channel/reseller motion
- Potential acquisition diligence pathway
