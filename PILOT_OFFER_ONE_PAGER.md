# DemandOrchestrator — Pilot Offer (One Pager)

> Working draft for outbound use. Keep aligned with FULL_BLUEPRINT.md.

## Pilot Objective
Validate DemandOrchestrator in a real operating environment and prove **risk-adjusted publishing speed** for your team.

## Ideal Pilot Customer
- Multi-person marketing/social team
- Needs governance, not just scheduling
- Concerned about publishing mistakes, role confusion, or compliance exposure

## Pilot Duration
**2–4 weeks**

## Pilot Scope
1. Workspace setup + account-type configuration
2. Channel connection (priority platforms)
3. Role and publisher authorization setup
4. Approval + publish flow activation
5. Reporting on execution metrics

## Success Metrics
- Reduction in time from idea to approved publish
- Reduction in publish errors/rework
- Consistency of role-based workflow execution
- Stakeholder confidence in auditability/governance

## Pilot Deliverables
- Configured production-like workspace
- Approved governance policy map
- Weekly progress summary
- End-of-pilot outcome report + rollout recommendation

## Commercial Frame (Draft)
- **Pilot fee:** fixed setup + enablement fee
- **Platform fee (during pilot):** discounted monthly
- **Conversion credit:** pilot fee partially credited to annual contract on conversion

## Post-Pilot Paths
1. **Scale rollout** (department or company-wide)
2. **Extended pilot** (additional teams/channels)
3. **Strategic partnership track** (co-development / channel collaboration)

## Why This Pilot Structure Works
- Fast time-to-value
- Low decision friction
- Clear quantitative outcomes
- Immediate path to broader deployment
