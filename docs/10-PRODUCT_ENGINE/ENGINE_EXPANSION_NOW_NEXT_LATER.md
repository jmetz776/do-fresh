# DemandOrchestrator Engine Expansion — Now / Next / Later

Date: 2026-03-07
Goal: Prioritize additional platforms + content models for fastest GTM and product lift.

## Prioritization Framework
- **Revenue impact**: direct pipeline/customer value
- **Distribution impact**: reach + discoverability
- **Build complexity**: integration + UX + moderation risk
- **Operational load**: support/compliance/maintenance burden

---

## NOW (0–30 days)
Focus: high leverage, low-to-medium complexity, immediate GTM lift.

### Platforms
1. **Newsletter / Email as a channel**
   - Why now: extends value beyond social; improves owned audience conversion.
   - Win: “one workflow → social + email.”

2. **Google Business Profile (if local/service ICP)**
   - Why now: high intent traffic and credibility for local buyer journeys.
   - Win: practical differentiation for SMB use cases.

### Content Models / Modes
1. **Repurpose Engine (1 source → multi-channel variants)**
   - Why now: directly demonstrates engine value and saves major time.
   - Deliverable: source post/video/script => X, LinkedIn, IG caption, short script, email snippet.

2. **Carousel/Document mode (LinkedIn + IG)**
   - Why now: high-performing format for B2B + educational content.
   - Deliverable: slide outline + copy + design notes.

3. **Quality Gate model before publish**
   - Why now: improves trust and reduces weak outputs.
   - Checks: brand fit, clarity, compliance, originality, CTA quality.

---

## NEXT (30–90 days)
Focus: medium complexity with strong growth/discoverability upside.

### Platforms
1. **Reddit**
   - Why next: massive demand capture if handled natively and carefully.
   - Risk: tone mismatch/spam perception; needs community-aware policy.

2. **Pinterest**
   - Why next: evergreen search-discovery loop, strong for visual/how-to niches.

3. **Threads**
   - Why next: easy incremental distribution once text/image flows are robust.

### Content Models / Modes
1. **Long-form SEO content mode**
   - Landing pages, blog posts, comparison pages, FAQ clusters.
   - Supports SEO + AEO/LLM discoverability roadmap.

2. **UGC brief + creator script mode**
   - Output creator-ready script packs and hook variants.

3. **Performance predictor ranking model**
   - Rank drafts pre-publish based on historical signal fit.

---

## LATER (90+ days)
Focus: higher complexity/maintenance, strategic expansion.

### Platforms
1. **App Store iOS companion (review/approve/queue/alerts first)**
2. **Facebook Pages/Groups deeper automations**
3. **Additional region-specific channels as ICP dictates**

### Content Models / Modes
1. **Comment/reply copilot** (engagement operations)
2. **Creative strategy autopilot** (campaign-level planning + testing)
3. **Advanced multimodal narrative system** (cross-post story continuity)

---

## Recommended Build Order (Practical)
1. Repurpose engine (NOW)
2. Quality gate pre-publish scoring (NOW)
3. Carousel/document output mode (NOW)
4. Long-form SEO/AEO mode (NEXT)
5. Reddit/Pinterest channel expansion (NEXT)
6. iOS companion decision gate + MVP (LATER unless GTM data pulls it forward)

---

1. Define minimum contracts for Repurpose Engine output objects.
2. Add quality-gate score schema and pass/fail thresholds.
3. Spec carousel mode output structure (headline, per-slide copy, CTA).
4. Create KPI dashboard sections for new modes (time saved, publish rate, quality score, conversion impact).
