# Partner Readiness Scorecard v1

Date: 2026-03-07
Owner: BD + CFO + Product
Purpose: Determine when DO is ready for different partnership outreach tiers.

## Scoring model
- 0 = not started
- 1 = partial/in progress
- 2 = strong/ready

Total score bands:
- 0–10: Foundation stage (prepare, do not push major negotiations)
- 11–18: Early outreach stage (intro calls + discovery)
- 19–24: Formal negotiation stage (pricing/SLA/co-marketing)

---

## 1) Usage Trajectory (max 6)
1. Weekly active workspaces trend documented (0/1/2)
2. Content generation volume by mode tracked (0/1/2)
3. 60-day growth forecast with assumptions (0/1/2)

## 2) Operational Maturity (max 6)
4. Reliability metrics available (success, retry, failure rates) (0/1/2)
5. Compliance safeguards implemented (consent, suppression, unsubscribe, audits) (0/1/2)
6. Production-readiness scorecard process in place (0/1/2)

## 3) Commercial Readiness (max 6)
7. Unit economics model populated with current assumptions (0/1/2)
8. Spend forecast by provider category (LLM/voice/video/infra) (0/1/2)
9. Negotiation asks and non-exclusivity position documented (0/1/2)

## 4) GTM Leverage (max 6)
10. Clear category narrative + messaging consistency (0/1/2)
11. Partner-facing deck ready (0/1/2)
12. Co-marketing plan and proof assets (demo, case narratives) (0/1/2)

---

## Stage gates

### Gate A: Intro Outreach Ready
Requirements:
- Score >= 11
- Compliance safeguards score >= 1 on all operational items
- Partner deck draft complete

### Gate B: Pricing Negotiation Ready
Requirements:
- Score >= 19
- Unit economics model filled with baseline numbers
- Usage trend and projected spend ranges available

### Gate C: Strategic Co-Sell Ready
Requirements:
- Score >= 21
- Stable product demo path
- Published platform/channel matrix and proof of output quality

---

## Evidence checklist (attach before outreach)
- GTM roadmap
- Unit economics sheet
- Production readiness scorecard
- Platform/channel matrix
- Partnership deck
- Security/compliance one-pager
