Business summary

- We are adding AI presenter video features and a presenter directory.
- Initial launch path: synthetic presenters first, then a licensed real-human presenter marketplace.

Planned commercial model
1) Subscription tiers

- Customers license presenter likeness.
- Platform takes fee; presenter receives payout.

Core legal/compliance questions
1) Likeness licensing
- Contract structure for consent, permitted use categories, blocked categories, geography, term, revocation, and takedowns.

2) Deception/deepfake controls
- Required disclosures and prohibited-use language (impersonation, deceptive endorsements, etc.).

3) Advertising and platform policy compliance

4) Privacy/biometric handling

5) IP/content risk
- UGC rights, output ownership/license language, DMCA/takedown workflow, repeat infringer policy.

6) Liability allocation
- Indemnity split between platform, customer, and model.
- Recommended insurance posture prior to marketplace launch.

Planned product guardrails
- Allowed/prohibited use matrix per presenter
- Policy checks before render/publish
- Human approval before posting
- Full audit logs per generation/render
- Immediate kill switch/revocation support

Requested deliverables

- Redlines for Terms, Privacy, Model Agreement, Acceptable Use Policy
