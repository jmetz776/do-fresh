# Pricing & Usage Transparency (Draft)

## Plans (initial)

### Starter
- Best for solo builders and early-stage teams
- Auto model mode (recommended)
- Advanced model selection: **not enabled**
- Included monthly AI usage budget: **$10**
- Hard cap: **$25**

### Pro
- Best for active creators/operators
- Auto or Advanced model mode
- Included monthly AI usage budget: **$50**
- Hard cap: **$120**

### Growth
- Best for teams running high volume
- Auto or Advanced model mode
- Includes best-tier model access
- Included monthly AI usage budget: **$200**
- Hard cap: **$500**

## Usage protection
- We track generation cost by model/provider in real time.
- At ~80% of included monthly budget, users receive warnings.
- At hard cap, generation is blocked or downgraded based on plan policy.

## Why this is good for customers
- Clear expectations before usage spikes.
- Freedom to choose better models on higher tiers.
- No surprise overages without visibility.

## Notes
Final dollar pricing for plans (what customers pay) should be set after invoice reconciliation and margin validation.
