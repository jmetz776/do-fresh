# Video CTA Polish Pack (Pass #2)

Use these endings to lift message clarity/CTA from 7 -> 9.

## CTA options
1. "If you want this exact workflow in your business, join early access and I’ll send the setup."
2. "Comment \"DO\" and I’ll send the playbook we use to turn one idea into pipeline content."
3. "Want this running for your team? Join the waitlist and I’ll send a 5-minute walkthrough."
4. "If consistency is your bottleneck, this is your fix—join early access and get the system."
5. "Ready to ship content without chaos? Get early access and I’ll show you the exact flow."

## Script tightening rules
- Keep final CTA sentence <= 16 words.
- Include one concrete next step (comment/join/watch).
- No vague phrases like "learn more".
- Keep spoken cadence natural (one breath per line).
